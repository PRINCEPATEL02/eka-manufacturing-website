About Us

Gujarat's Leading Industrial Brush Manufacturer

EKA Manufacturing is a premier manufacturer and exporter of industrial brushes based in Visnagar, Gujarat. We specialize in high-quality brushes used in manufacturing processes and sweeping machines (boomer machines).

Our products are crafted from premium HDPE and Polypropylene materials, ensuring exceptional durability and performance in demanding industrial environments. We serve clients across India with consistent quality and competitive pricing.
